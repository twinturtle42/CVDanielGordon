Me

I'm a Sociologist that shifted to building web applications using Ruby on Rails.

After graduating in Sociology on a thesis regarding young entrepreneurs in Shenzhen (considered the Silicon Valley of hardware) my interest in the the tech- and startup scene was sparked. Upon following a traineeship towards social entrepreneurship and starting my own social startup I decided I wanted to learn how to code. Le Wagon Dev bootcamp gave me the tools to work in a tech-company as a developer or to take my own startup to the next level.
